How It Works

From Studio to Stardom

01

Create with Mudaber Ghost

Don't have a track yet? No problem. Use Mudaber Ghost to hire a professional producer. You'll get a fully mixed and mastered track tailored to your vision, complete with 100% of the commercial rights. It's your music.

02

Distribute via Mudaber Art

Take your finished track to Mudaber Art. Upload your audio and artwork, set your release date, and we'll distribute it to Spotify, Apple Music, TikTok, and over 40 other major DSPs globally in as little as 24 hours.

03

Scale with Mudaber Tech

Use Mudaber Tech's suite of tools to build your professional artist website, manage your digital marketing campaigns, and analyze your global streaming data. Turn your listeners into a dedicated, monetized fanbase.
