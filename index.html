What We Do

Control your career without compromise.

We built Mudaber to give independent artists the same power as major labels without restrictive contracts, opaque royalties, or loss of creative ownership.

Work With Us

01 Music Distribution

Release on Spotify, Apple Music, YouTube Music, Amazon, Deezer, TikTok, and 40+ platforms. Fast delivery, real-time analytics, 100% royalties.

02 Publishing & Rights

Copyright registration, royalty tracking, and media licensing. Your music stays yours — we protect and monetize your intellectual property.

03 Artist Management

Full career support from branding and marketing to commercial licensing and campaign planning. Over 500 artists trust Mudaber.

04 Ghost Production

Access a network of world-class producers via Mudaber Ghost. Commission original tracks and receive full usage rights for commercial releases.

Proprietary platforms for release management, analytics, royalties, and contracts — all in one dashboard powered by artificial intelligence.

Content strategy, campaign management, social media growth, and data-driven promotion to turn listeners into a loyal, engaged fanbase.

About Mudaber

Powering the future of independent music.

Mudaber is a dynamic music company founded to amplify the voices of musicians and provide fertile ground for creativity. With over 500 artists, sound engineers, and DJs, Mudaber creates a powerful symphony of creativity that resonates beyond traditional boundaries.

We stand as a bridge between independent record labels and talented music artists worldwide — supporting everything from global distribution to the birth of new genres like JAMAX (hard techno mixed with dubstep and hardstyle).
