Our Story

Empowering the independent music scene since 2015. We are Mudaber.

The Vision

Music without limits.

Mudaber was founded on a simple principle: artists should have the power of a major label without giving up their rights or creative control.

Started in London by Jamal Belal, we have grown into a massive ecosystem encompassing distribution (Mudaber Art), ghost production (Mudaber Ghost), and technological innovations (Mudaber Tech). We are not just participating in the music industry; we are rebuilding its infrastructure.
