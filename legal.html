Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 2026

At Mudaber Ltd, we take your privacy seriously. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal data across the Mudaber ecosystem (Mudaber Art, Mudaber Ghost, Mudaber Tech).

1. Information We Collect

We may collect personal information such as your name, email address, payment details, and IP address when you register for our services, distribute music, or contact support.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide, maintain, and improve our services.
  • To process transactions and send related information (e.g., royalty payouts).
  • To send administrative messages, security alerts, and technical notices.

3. Data Security

We use industry-standard encryption protocols (SSL/TLS) to ensure that your data remains secure during transmission. We do not sell your personal data to third parties.
